<section class="section section-faq">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="section-header">
      <span class="section-label">FAQ</span>
      <h2>Common Questions from Product & Support Teams</h2>
    </div>
    <div class="faq-grid">
      <div class="faq-item">
        <h3>Do I need to know how to code to use this?</h3>
        <p>No. Probe is designed for non-technical users. You interact through natural language queries and pre-built workflow templates. The system handles the technical complexity of understanding code, architecture, and dependencies - you just ask questions and get grounded answers.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="faq-item">
        <h3>How is this different from just asking ChatGPT about our product?</h3>
        <p>ChatGPT has no access to your actual systems. Probe connects directly to your code, docs, tickets, and customer data - giving you answers grounded in reality, not hallucinations. Every answer includes evidence trails showing exactly where the information came from.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="faq-item">
        <h3>Will engineering need to help set this up?</h3>
        <p>Initial connection to your systems may require brief engineering involvement (similar to setting up any integration). After that, Product and Support teams can use Probe independently. Workflow templates are ready to use out of the box.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="faq-item">
        <h3>Can I customize the workflows for our process?</h3>
        <p>Yes. Workflow templates can be customized to match your team's structure, terminology, and quality requirements. You can define your own quality gates, spec templates, and triage rules without writing code.</p>
      </div>
